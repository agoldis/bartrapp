Discover Bartr
Bartr aims to create continuous exchanges of services within communities that promote a new, shared economy. The purpose of Bartr is to encourage people to offer their skills to others around them, while giving them access to the skills of others for them to benefit from.
STRENGTHEN YOUR COMMUNITY
Bartr provides communities with a platform for their members to mutually benefit from one another's skills, strengthening existing bonds, whilst also enabling new friendships to blossom.
SAVE MONEY
Bartr takes us back to a time of giving and getting. Bartr redefines value as a possession of skills, knowledge and new experiences with those around us.
BE SOCIAL
By taking your online interactions into the offline world, Bartr is all about creating quality time where people can gain new skills from new friends.
About

Bartr is a platform that enables people to exchange their skills and services with others in their community. Through a system of virtual credits, Bartr allows you to use your skills and knowledge as a means to obtain services that interest you from others.

Bartr was created by Tanya Cohen and Maxime Dahan, who founded the project in April 2013, during the final year of their studies at the IDC, in Herzliya, Israel. After completing their Communications BA degrees, both Tanya and Maxime participated in the 6-month HIP accelerator program hosted by Heseg Foundation and Presentense.
